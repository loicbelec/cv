Current Mandate

At Alpine Racing, I lead the design and evolution of the data platform that connects on-premise operational systems with Azure-based services supporting Formula 1 and endurance programs.

The mandate is broader than infrastructure. It includes architectural governance, modernization priorities, cloud cost structure, metadata strategy and the practical operating model required to turn platform capability into usable business and engineering outcomes.

The core challenge is disciplined complexity management: preserving speed for engineering teams while introducing the controls expected from enterprise-grade systems.
